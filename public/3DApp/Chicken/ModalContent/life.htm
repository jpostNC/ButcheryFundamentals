Chickens are raised in a number of ways:

  • They can be bred hatched and raised on a single farm,
  • They can be raised in a broiler farm where the purpose is to produce meat birds
  • They can be raised in a pullet farm where they are raised to be breeding roosters and hens.
  • They can be laid in a breeder farm and then transferred to another farm (like a broiler farm) to be hatched and raised.
  • Broilers: They are raised for 6-8 weeks depending on the weight requested by the market they usually between 2-3 kilos.
