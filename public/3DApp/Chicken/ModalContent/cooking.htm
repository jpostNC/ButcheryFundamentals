Young, tender birds like broilers, fryers and cornish hens are usually cooked by dry-heat methods, such as grilling, broiling, frying, and roasting.

Older, tougher birds need slow, moist heat to be made palatable such as stewing, poaching or braising
Low temp roasting is best for larger birds, such as turkeys & capons
