Inspection and Grading

Inspection: All Poultry is subject to federal inspection Provides a guarantee of wholesomeness and fit for human consumption Inspection is required by Canadian law.

Grading is voluntary:

There are 3 grades of Poultry.

Raised without antibiotics: Chickens are not given any antibiotics in their feed. If a bird gets ill and requires an antibiotic they cannot be sold under the “raised without antibiotics” certification. These are marketing terms and are unregulated and not certified.
