Free Range: Chicken that has access to the outdoors and pasture.

Free Run: Chickens that are not raised in cages but can freely roam in a barn.

Hormone Free/ Steroid Free: Hormone and steroid use in poultry farming are illegal in Canada.

Kosher: “The Hebrew word “kosher” means fit or proper as it relates to Jewish dietary law. Kosher foods are permitted to be eaten, and can be used as ingredients in the production of additional food items.” For further information on Kosher visit UO Kosher. https://oukosher.org/the-kosher-primer/

Halal: “This is an Arabic term which means permissible or lawful in Islam. In reference to food, it is the Islamic dietary standard, as prescribed in the Shari'ah (Islamic Law).” https://halalhmc.org/resources/definition-of-halal/

For further information visit Halal Monitoring Authority Canada. https://hmacanada.org/

Organic: a certification that is regulated by the Canadian General Standards Board. Essentially means the animals are fed organic feed (no animal by products) & has been raised without the use of antibiotics or non-organically certified supplements

Raised without antibiotics: Asserts that chickens are not given any antibiotics in their feed. If a bird gets ill and requires an antibiotic they cannot be sold under the “raised without antibiotics,” certification.
