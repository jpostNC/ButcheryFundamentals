Roaster: The largest available size of chicken

Broiler/Fryer: The most common food service size of chicken, weighs between 2-3 kilos

Cornish Hen: A small chicken 500-750g

Capon: Castrated male chicken, can be similar size as roaster

Fowl/Stewing Hen: A large bird used after it stops producing eggs usually reserved for stewing since it will be tougher.
