Poultry

The flesh of poultry and game birds is muscle tissue

Muscles consist of muscle fibers held together in bundles by connective tissue - Information taken from our PPT- Muscle tissue is composed of: Water (about 75%) Protein (about 20%) Fat (up to 5%) & Other elements, including carbohydrate, in small quantities

Majority of chickens raised in Canada for their meat are:

Plymouth White Rock

Cornish White Rock

Other birds commonly consumed: Turkey, duck, goose, guinea, pigeon (squab)

Game birds: quail, partridges, pheasant, wild duck
