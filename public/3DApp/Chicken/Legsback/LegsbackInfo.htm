Bone pocket: place in meat where a bone has been removed

Femur: The bone that holds the thigh meat in one place.

Hip joint: (poultry) area on backbone where the thigh bone connects

Leg: the lower part of a bird, it contains the thigh and drumstick. Can be purchased with part of the backbone attached. Located at the bottom of the chicken leg. Contains elastin and thicker connective tissue.

Oyster: Located on either side of the backbone near the thighs. Oyster shaped muscle. Very tender.

Thigh: The meatiest part of the leg, it is full of collagen, and carries more flavour than the breast. It is located on the top part of the leg attached to the backbone.
