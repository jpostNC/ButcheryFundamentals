Femur: The femur & humerus bones are large bones from the leg they are full of marrow and can be split to be served as marrow bones. They add lots of flavour to stocks.

Heel muscles: muscle from the heel of the animal, high gelatin content, popular cuts in various parts of the world - overlooked in North America

Shank (Beef): A locomotive muscle, the shank is often used to propel the animal forward or backwards, it is tough and full of sinew and collagen. It requires long cooking. Cooking methods recommended with this cut are braising, pressure cooking, sous- vide.
