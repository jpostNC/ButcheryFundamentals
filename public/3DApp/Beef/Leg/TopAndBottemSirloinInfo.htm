Beef Tallow: Rendered beef fat

Bottom sirloin: Beef sub primal cut, composed of tri tip - flank and flap meat

Flank: Primal cut, surrounds abdomen. Fabricated cut: flank steak

Patella: beef) It is the kneecap attached to the sirloin tip.

Sirloin: Large muscle from the very back of the long loin section. It is very tender and very lean. (Sanagans, 2020) Cooking methods recommended with this cut are pan -frying, grilling, pan-broil or sous vide

Suet: is the raw, hard fat of beef, lamb or mutton found around the loins and kidney

Tri tip: triangular cut of meat from the bottom sirloin
