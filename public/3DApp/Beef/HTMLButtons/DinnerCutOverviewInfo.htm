Baseball Steak: Steak cut, part of the beef top sirloin. The baseball steak comes from the center cut. It is known for its lean composition and flavour. Comes from the medallion that is located in the top butt.

Beef Ribeye: Cut from the rib of beef, the bone and tail has been removed. Can be a steak or a roast.

Beef Tenderloin: Most tender cut of beef, located under the loin in the belly cavity, it starts at the ribs and runs into the leg. It is further cut into: “Filet Mignon”- center cut steaks; “Chateau Briand”, the head of the tender loin which sits in the leg; and the tail portion of the tenderloin which are called 'Tournedos”

Beef: meat from cattle used for food

Boneless rib steak: has the tail and fat still attached, the ribeye, would be the eye only.

Grass fed red veal: Veal raised on grass

Ossobuco: Italian dish made out of the beef shank, often braised at a low temperature.

Porterhouse Steak: Cut from the rear part of the beef short loin, the porterhouse consists of a bone in a form of “T “a large piece of the tenderloin on one side and a piece of the striploin on the other.

PROVIMI: veal stands for Proteins, Vitamins and Minerals. Provimi veal is raised indoors on a liquid diet supplement created to replicate the benefits of mother's milk. Provimi veal are usually young male dairy calves, and Provimi veal does exist as a by product of the dairy industry

Sirloin cap steak: Steak cut from the sirloin, also known as “pichana”

Sirloin steak: steak cut from the sirloin

Striploin: Can be a roast or a steak. Cut from the short loin on beef, it is a supportive muscle, so it is very tender. This is commonly known as a New York Steak.

Tenderloin: The most tender cut on any animal. On 4 legged animals the tenderloin muscle starts at the ribs and runs into the leg. It is connected to the primal loin cut of beef and pork and the primal saddle cut of lamb.

Top sirloin heart or butt: part of top sirloin - largest of the muscles that make up top sirloin - when cut it makes what is known as heart steaks

Veal chops: veal rib chops cut from the rib primal, very popular restaurant cut.

Veal tenderloin: most tender cut of veal. See tenderloin

Veal: refers to the meat of males calves of dairy cattle breeds
