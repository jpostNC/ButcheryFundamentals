Clod (Beef): cut from the Chuck (shoulder) The clod contains other roast cuts, like the top blade and the flat iron. Since it is a locomotive muscle group it is medium tender with lots of flavour.

Silverskin: Is a thin membrane of connective tissue that exists on some muscles. Silverskin tissue gets tougher with age.

To feather off (a piece of meat): slang for cleaning off connective tissue
