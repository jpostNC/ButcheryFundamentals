Angus: Breed of meat cow

Bull: mature male cattle used for breeding

Carcass: In butchery carcass refers to the body of an animal after it has been slaughtered and eviscerated Grass fed

Charolais: Breed of meat cow

Cow: A mature cattle female, that has had offspring, used for milk or procreation

Foragers: (in animals) to search and or dig for food using their snout

Haylage: dried hay

Heifer: A young mature female cattle used for breeding, that has not had a calf

Hereford: Breed of meat cow

Silage: fermented hay

Simental: Breed of meat cow

Steer: A desexed male cattle that will not be used for creating offspring, but for meat
