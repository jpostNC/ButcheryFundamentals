Boar: (type) a tusked Eurasian wild pig. Boar is referred to as Wild- however, it is raised in captivity to meet federal and provincial inspection standards.

Loin: cut of meat that runs on either side of the spine, very tender, support muscle.

Saddle: A tender cut of meat, most often rabbit, lamb, veal or venison that is the unseparated loin from the rib to the leg, from both sides of an animal. Contains, striploin, middle and tail end of the tenderloin and part of the backbone. Classically the saddle is served boneless, stuffed with a farce, trussed and roasted. Very tender cut great for grilling, roasting, pan searing, poaching.
