Deer Rack: Refers to the rack of ribs but can also mean antlers.

Dry heat cooking method: a method of cooking using dry heat through conduction or convection the heat can come from under above or around, common methods are grilling, sauteing, pan searing, frying, roasting, baking.

Rack of Lamb: Comprised of seven or eight ribs and from the center of the animal. Very tender cut great for grilling, roasting, pan searing.
