Center bone: describes spine on flatfish

Fillet: (Noun) A boneless piece of fish, usually a whole side.

Fillet: (Verb): To remove the bones from a piece of fish

Fish Stock: liquid made by simmering fish bones with vegetables, used as a base for fish soups and sauces.

Flatfish: A flat fish, typically bottom feeders, that have 4 fillets. Both eyes are on the topside of the head.

Score: make an incision that is not very deep, used to create a guiding line for deep cuts.
