Beak: a part of an octopus, like the beak of a parrot, used for cracking shellfish

Calamari: A culinary name for dishes made from any type of squid, typically deep fried or grilled.

Quill: Translucid backbone, found inside the squid. Holds the structure of the squid. This is the feather-shaped internal structure that supports the squid's mantle and is a place for muscles to attach.

Squid Ink: Dark colour ink usually used for defense mechanisms by octopus and squid.
