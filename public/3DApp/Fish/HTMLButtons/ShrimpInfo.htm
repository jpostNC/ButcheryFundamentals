16/20: Measure used to indicate number per lb. 16/20 is 16 to 20 shrimp per pound. Used for shrimp and scallops.

Crustacean: Shellfish that have jointed legs and segmented shells.

Devein: The act of removing the main central vein from a shrimp or prawn.

Prawn: Crustacean that resembles a large shrimp.

Shell: hard protective case around a mollusk or crustacean

Shrimp: A small free-swimming crustacean with an elongated body, frequently harvested for food.

Stock: (crustacean stock), flavourful liquid made by simmering shellfish shells with vegetables, water, and other flavour enhancers. Can be used for making bisque.
