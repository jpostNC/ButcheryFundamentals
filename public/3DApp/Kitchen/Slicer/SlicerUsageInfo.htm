To Disassemble Slicer

  1. Turn the dial to 0 and confirm the blade is hidden.
  2. Unplug the machine.
  3. Remove food tray by unscrewing screws
  4. Unscrew plate that covers the blade
  5. Wash and sanitize the food tray and plate
  6. Wash the slicer with hot soap and water, dry and then sanitize it.

To assemble slicer

  1. Attach the blade plate and screw it back in.
  2. Attach the food tray and screw it back in.
