Dial: Indicator on slicer used to determine thickness of the slice. The smaller the number the thinner the slice.

Food Tray (Slicer): part on deli slicer used to hold the product being sliced.

Guard: Protective plate on deli slicer to protect hand from blade

Instructions for Using Slicer.

  1. Make sure the machine is turned off and the dial is adjusted to 0 so there is no exposed blade.
  2. Slide the guard up and place the food product on the food tray and press the guard onto the product.
  3. Turn dial to desired thickness setting
  4. Turn machine on
  5. Push meat onto the slicer blade by using guard handles provided.
  6. NEVER OPERATE A SLICER WITHOUT USING THE GUARD. PEOPLE LOSE FINGERS AND GET HORRIBLE CUTS BY DOING THIS.
  7. Slice your product.
  8. When you have completed the slicing, turn the machine off and unplug it before washing it.
