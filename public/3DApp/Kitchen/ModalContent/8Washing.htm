It is important to follow municipal guidelines for washing and sanitizing dishes and tools.

The 3 sink washing method is the preferred method by most food safety inspectors and they will test your water temperature and sanitization solutions. Make sure that you keep test strips with your sanitizers and test it according to the manufacturer's instructions. It is a good manufacturing procedure to keep a log of this as well.

Step 1.
Scrape waste into a bin or compost
Step 2.
In Sink 1: Wash dishes in hot soapy water
Step 3
In Sink 2: Rinse dishes in clean water at 43℃ 110℉
Step 4
In Sink 3. Sanitize in water at least 77℃ 170℉ with iodine mixture to make 25ppm or with a quaternary ammonium mixture at 200 ppm.
Step 5
Air Dry

For more information contact your public health unit.
