Mechanical tenderizer: (Delicator): a Mechanical meat tenderizer is a piece of equipment used to tenderize meat.

Schnitzel: Thinly pounded and tenderized cutlet. Breaded and fried. Usually cut from leg muscles. Most often used with pork, can also be used with beef and veal.

Tenderize meat: the process of breaking down the muscle fibres in a tough cut of meat to make it more tender and thinner. This can be achieved with a meat mallet which hammers the meat, or a mechanical tenderizer which makes many small incisions in the meat.

Mallet: resembles a hammer, used to tenderize meat. One side has large dimples and one has smaller dimples, one side can also be flat.

Schnitzel: Thinly pounded and tenderized cutlet. Breaded and fried. Usually cut from leg muscles. Most often used with pork, can also be used with beef and veal. German name. Scallopini in Italian.

How to assemble mechanical tenderizer:

  1. Attach guiding arms across the top of the blades . There are two guiding arms. Normally letters are found in equipment to help with this process - A+B, B+A.
  2. Clip both halves together
  3. Once attached, slide into meat tenderizer
  4. Place guard over the blades, tenderizer will not work until the guard is in proper place
  5. Plug in equipment
