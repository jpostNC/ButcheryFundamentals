Gelatin: comes from collagen- it is extracted from animal skin bones and provides gel to stocks and sauces. It can be further processed into a powder and used as a gelling agent. It is clear and flavourless.

Bone fragment: a shard of bone, usually from a saw or cleaver.

Scraper: tool used to scrape bone fragments off of meat

Bone Marrow: tissue that exists in the middle of bones, it adds flavour to stocks and soups and can be eaten. Bone marrow from beef femur bones are a delicacy.

Beef Shin: British term for shank, same applies for veal.

Band saw: electric saw, with a band-style blade, used for cutting bones and steaks in a butcher shop. Meat is usually pre cut to the bone before going on the band saw.

Mesh vest: protective metal vest used in industrial meat cutting plant to protect butchers from knife cuts.

Safety goggles: protective eyewear worn to protect eyes from sharp objects or harmful liquids.

Ossobuco: Italian dish made from the beef shank, often braised at a low temperature.
