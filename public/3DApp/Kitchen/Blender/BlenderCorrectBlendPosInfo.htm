Grinder Blade: a + (cross) shaped cutter with 4 sides, one of each of the 4 sides is sharpened into a blade. The sharpened side presses up against the grinder plate. When the auger spins it pushes the meat against the grinder plate and the grinder blade cuts it and forces it through the plate and the end result is ground meat.

To grind: The process of cutting meat into very small dice. Sometimes called mincing.

Ground meat: the end result of grinding meat.

Schmear: (smear) an undesirable result of meat and fat being ground together where the fat melts and smears. Usually occurs if a grinder is not assembled correctly or if it gets stuck with bone fragments.
