Ball joint: bone head that resembles a ball, as in ball and socket joint.

Blood clots: blood that did not come out of animal - needs to be removed to avoid odd flavours in final product

Boneless shoulder (in Lamb): the deboned shoulder, usually tied for roasting

Connective tissue: Connects the skeletal muscles to the bones, it also gives structure to other tissues or organs in the body.

Keel bone (in Lamb): resembles shark fin is part of the underside of the scapula

Scapula: also known as the shoulder blade, connects to the arm bone.
