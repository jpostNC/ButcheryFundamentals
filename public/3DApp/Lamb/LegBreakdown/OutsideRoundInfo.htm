Eye of Round: Muscle that comes from the leg, it is lean but tough. Cooking methods recommended with this cut are grilling, roasting, braising

Knuckle or Sirloin Tip: located at the crest of the leg and connects to the saddle. Tender cut, great for grilling, serve medium rare.

Sirloin: Large muscle from the very back of the long loin section. It is very tender and very lean. (Sanagans, 2020) Cooking methods recommended with this cut are pan -frying, grilling, pan-broil or sous vide
