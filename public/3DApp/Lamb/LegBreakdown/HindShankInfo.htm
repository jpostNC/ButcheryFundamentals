Achilles Tendon: a very strong tendon at the heel of the animal. This is where hooks are inserted to hang animals after slaughter

Connective tissue: Connects the skeletal muscles to the bones, it also gives structure to other tissues or organs in the body.

Frenching: removing all connective tissue from bone ends. This is done to improve the aesthetic of the bone, to make it look more elegant.

Lamb Hind shank: shank cut from the back leg. Best for braising or grinding.
