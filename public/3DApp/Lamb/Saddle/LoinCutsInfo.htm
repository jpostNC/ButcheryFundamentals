Band saw: electric saw, with a band-style blade, used for cutting bones and steaks in a butcher shop

Chine bone: Back bone which is attached to the rib section of the animal

Lamb Chops: Contains a portion of the loin and the tenderloin and is a lean product that cooks quickly. Very tender cut great for grilling, roasting, pan searing. Comparable to T-bone steaks in beef

Lamb Loin: Primal cut also known as the saddle, contains loin, and parts of the tenderloin and vertebrate.

Striploin: Can be a roast or a steak. Cut from the short loin on beef, it is a supportive muscle, so it is very tender. This is commonly known as a New York Steak.

Tenderloin: The most tender cut on any animal. On 4 legged animals the tenderloin muscle starts at the ribs and runs into the leg. It is connected to the primal loin cut of beef and pork and the primal saddle cut of lamb.
