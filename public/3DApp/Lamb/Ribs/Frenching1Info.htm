Connective tissue: Connects the skeletal muscles to the bones, it also gives structure to other tissues or organs in the body.

Knife Frenching:

  1. Measure and draw a line across the rack where you will be Frenching
  2. Score the back of the bones then remove bone skin (connective tissue) so you can get underneath it
  3. Use your knife to scrape down the meat
  4. Cut straight line of excess meat off
  5. Use your knife to scrape any connective tissue left on bones
