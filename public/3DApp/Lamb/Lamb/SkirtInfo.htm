Diaphragm Muscle: A muscle located in the belly portion of the animal, often referred to as the skirt steaks in retail cuts.

Flank: see Skirt Steak

Skirt Steak: Located on the inside of the abdomen wall just below the ribs, skirt steak can be either of two long, flat, well-marbled muscles: the diaphragm and the abdominal muscle. A little rough to look at, skirt steaks are versatile and full of flavor.
