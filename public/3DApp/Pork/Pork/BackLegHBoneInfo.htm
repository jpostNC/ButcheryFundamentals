Bone: Living tissue that make up the skeleton of bodies, contains gelatin and other flavourful compounds

Femoral head: Head of the femur (thigh) bone

Femur: The bone that holds the thigh meat in one place.

Reverse grip or pistol grip or dagger grip: a way of holding a knife by clenching the handle so the blade points down towards the floor, holding a blade this way is an effective way of manipulating the tip of the blade around round bones.

Spider steak: It's a little semi-circle weaved steak with a web of intramuscular fat that sits inside the hip on the Aitch bone, and there are only two per steer. It's small, flavourful thanks to its natural marbling.

Tendon: It is a ligament that attaches muscle to bone. It is tough and inedible.

Tracing the bone: following the bone with your knife tip
