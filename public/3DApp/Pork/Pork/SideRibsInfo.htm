Bacon: cured and smoked pork belly

Cartilage: It is a flexible connective tissue. Its main function is to help keep joints and other structural parts in one place

Curing: process of preservation and flavouring of food items.

Diaphragm Muscle: A muscle located in the belly portion of the animal, often referred to as the skirt steaks in retail cuts.

Side ribs: extension of back ribs off the loin -length and size determined by where you cut off the belly from the loin
