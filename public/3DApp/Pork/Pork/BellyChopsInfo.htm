Bone in belly chops: (pork) a specialty cut, when the belly is removed from the pig there are still ribs attached, a butcher can make a beautiful fatty belly chop with by cutting between the ribs through the belly, rather than removing the ribs and selling them separately as side ribs

Diaphragm muscle: Skirt Steak: Located on the inside of the abdomen wall just below the ribs, skirt steak can be either of two long, flat, well-marbled muscles: the diaphragm and the abdominal muscle. A little rough to look at, skirt steaks are versatile and full of flavour

Flank Steak: Steak, cut from the lower abdominal muscle of the cow, known as Bavette in France. It is a tough cut, with defined grains in the meat, and very flavourful.

Inside/outside skirt: inside is the skirt steak, outside is the flank steak
