Bone Saw: Butcher Saw, a hand saw used to cut through bone, not used for cutting meat.

Boning Knife: a butcher's knife with a blade that ranges from 6-12 inches, it has a curved tip and can be sturdy or flexible. It is used for removing meat from bone. It is not used to cut bone.

Carcass: In butchery carcass refers to the body of an animal after it has been slaughtered and eviscerated.

Eviscerated: refers to an animal or fish that has had their internal organs removed. It is important that this process is done as soon as possible after slaughter to prevent the organs from spoiling the muscles. Removing the organs also assists in cooling the carcass. Cooling is imperative to get the carcass out of the danger zone before harmful bacteria can breed.

Scimitar: A style of butcher knife used for cutting through large pieces of meat.
