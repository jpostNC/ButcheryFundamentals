Fascia: refers to connective tissue between muscles

Femur: The bone that holds the thigh meat in one place.

Gooseneck: A combination three muscles: bottom round, eye of round and heel

Inside Round: A large muscle from the inside of the leg. Medium tender cut, can be grilled or pan roasted, skewered or stewed.

Knuckle or Sirloin Tip: located at the crest of the leg and connects to the saddle. Tender cut, great for grilling, serve medium rare.
