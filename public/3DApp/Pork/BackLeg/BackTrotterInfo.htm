Prosciutto: an Italian dry-cured leg of pork.

Femur: The bone that holds the thigh meat in one place.

Reverse grip or pistol grip or dagger grip: a way of holding a knife by clenching the handle so the blade points down towards the floor, holding a blade this way is an effective way of manipulating the tip of the blade around round bones.

Trotter: refers to the foot of pig

Hock: a part of the pig, hock refers to the joint between the Tibia and Trotter

Tibia: is the bone between the patella (kneecap) and the trotter
